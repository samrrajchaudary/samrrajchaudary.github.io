Creativity and Empathy have always been at the helm of dictating career choices for me. During my schooling, I initiated my creative journey with graphic design and was always intrigued by seeing problems around me wanting to always solve them which resulted me entering the field of design as a medium to solve it. Took up as a hobby eventually turning out to be my career option. Having a deep affection for design and technology, I went onto & am currently pursuing my bachelor's in Design with a specialisation in UX . Adding to that I have been very fortunate to receive awards in multiple international/national competitions. Out of all, closest to my heart is a recent one at IITB. I believe that technology can revolutionise how people learn, communicate and create. I'm passionate about shaping products and services that help people to bridge gap between Human and technology. .
